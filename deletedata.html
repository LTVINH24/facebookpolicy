Delete User Data

If you wish to delete your personal data collected by our application when you logged in using Facebook, please follow the instructions below.

How to Request Data Deletion

  1. Send an email request to: softwaredesignstudentsupport@gmail.com.
  2. In your email, please include:
    • Full Name
    • Your Facebook Account ID (or the email you used to log in)
    • Message content: "Request to delete user data"
  3. We will confirm and process your request within 48 hours upon receipt.

Note

We only store basic user information permitted by Facebook, including your name, email, and profile picture upon login. After deletion, all data associated with you will be completely removed from our system.
